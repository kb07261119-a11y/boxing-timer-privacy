Common Questions

How do I enable the cornerman voice?

Open the app and tap Settings. Toggle on the Cornerman Voice option.

Can I customize round length and rest time?

Yes — tap Settings to adjust round duration, rest period, and number of rounds.

Does the app require internet?

No. The Boxing! works fully offline.

I purchased Unlock Forever but lost access after reinstalling.

Tap the Unlock button and select Restore Purchase to recover your purchase at no charge.
